Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from the Krishna Foods Ordering App, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to yashkadav52@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account".
  2. Include your registered Google account email in the message body.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • User profile information (name, phone, email, location).
  • Order history and related app activity.
  • Authentication credentials.

Data Retention

No user data will be retained after deletion, except where legally required (e.g., invoices kept for accounting and tax purposes).

If you have any questions, contact us at yashkadav52@gmail.com.
